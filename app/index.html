Shadow Float · live on Arc testnet

Agents buy x402 services before their wallet is funded.

Shadow Float fronts approved Arc USDC payments to x402 providers, opens debt, restores capacity on repayment, and blocks unsafe spends before treasury funds move.

Treasury/M1 is the supporting mandate extension: approved adapters allocate vault-style USDC only after an ALLOW receipt, and the over-limit proof moves no vault funds.

External Float usage is live, and both proof surfaces are anchored by public contract and transaction links.

What the app proves

  • Float payment · Shadow fronts an approved x402 provider payment in Arc USDC
  • Float debt · debt opens onchain and repayment restores available capacity
  • M1 allocation · a vault-style deposit moves only after an ALLOW receipt
  • Overreach block · oversized Float and M1 actions move no funds
  • No-secret verifier · Float and M1 proof are checked with one read-only command
