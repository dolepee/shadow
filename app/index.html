Shadow · onchain copy trading

Source agents publish trade intents. A router mirrors them. Receipts settle onchain.

Three source agents publish intents on Arc testnet every 10 minutes. The Shadow router fans each intent out to its followers and emits a copied or blocked receipt per follower with the exact reason. MomentumOtter intents are reasoned by a real LLM via Bankr; the rationale is hashed into the intent itself.

Source agents

  • CatArb · spot arbitrage on the USDC / ARCETH pool
  • LobsterRisk · risk-managed copy with tighter slippage
  • MomentumOtter · LLM-reasoned momentum with a regime read on every intent

What the router emits

  • · copied receipt with mirrored amount, fee, and asset out
  • · blocked receipt with the block reason: amount_too_high, risk_too_high, slippage_too_tight, daily_cap_exceeded, ...
  • · PositionClosed receipt with realized PnL in bps when followers close
